"TRYHARDS" SPOTTED AT ALNO ROCKY MOUNTAIN RESORT

The supposed "Tryhards" Group was witnessed at Alno Rocky Mountain Resort on the 9th of December 2025, just the week after they took their final exams and when their semester just ended. The group includes Freddie Visaya Jr., Peniel Ebes, Sebastian Damugo, Sean Earl Rosalejos, and Lorenz Calderon. They went on a resort to unwind and celebrate their hard-earned break, eager to soak up the sun, relax by the pool, and forget about textbooks and deadlines for a while. The group had been brewing this plan at the beginning of their first semester. At the day of scheduled event, the group was very eager that they woke up early to meet at Alapang Junction. After taking a ride, the group arrived at the resort. They experienced hilarous moments like struggling at igniting the cook place, pranking friends that are resting, and of course playing around the pool. Lorenz stated that "It was quite lucky that we went out exactly this day as I had somethig on my mind, but being in a new environment and being with friends made this one of the day of all time"
